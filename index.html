RECOVERY PROCESS

What happens after a vehicle goes missing.

A clear, sequential response your F&I team can walk a customer through at the point of sale.

01

Theft occurs

Safelink continues transmitting location even if the thief disconnects the battery, thanks to hidden, antenna-free installation.

02

Customer reports the vehicle stolen

The customer contacts the 24/7/365 recovery support line as soon as theft is discovered.

03

Recovery team engages

Cellular and GPS/satellite data are used to track the vehicle in real time, coast-to-coast.

04

Direct coordination with law enforcement

The recovery team works directly with police to recover the vehicle quickly and safely.

05

Guarantee backs the outcome

If the vehicle isn't recovered, the money-back guarantee protects the customer's investment.
