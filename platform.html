SMART INVENTORY MANAGEMENT

Track, manage, and optimize the lot without walking it.

Every Safelink-equipped unit reports its position, battery health, and last-known condition into one map view — so a lot check that used to take a walk-through takes a glance.

  • → Real-time vehicle location and status tracking
  • → Instant view of stock levels and battery health

SMART GEOFENCING
ENHANCE SECURITY WITH SMART GEOFENCING

Know the moment a unit leaves the lot.

Draw a custom geofence around the lot, a satellite storage yard, or an off-site auction line, and get an alert the instant a vehicle crosses the boundary — day or night.

  • → Custom geofences define safe zones per location
  • → Instant alerts on unauthorized movement
  • → Real-time visibility across the entire lot
  • → Stronger asset protection tied directly to inventory control

SMART OPERATING MODES

Adaptive tracking, tuned for battery efficiency.

Every device ships with three operating modes, so battery life is matched to the situation — long-term lot storage, active daily monitoring, or an active recovery.

BATTERY IMPACT — LOW

PowerSave Mode

Ultra-efficient mode for maximum battery life with 24-hour location updates. Ideal for units sitting in inventory.

BATTERY IMPACT — MEDIUM

Smart Trip Mode

Smart monitoring with instant alerts for movement or boundary breaches. Built for 24/7 peace of mind once a unit is sold.

BATTERY IMPACT — HIGH

Recover+ Mode

Enables real-time tracking to assist in stolen vehicle recovery. Recommended for emergency use only, once a theft is reported.
