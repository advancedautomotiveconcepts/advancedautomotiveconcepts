## 2025-07-16 - Accessibility Foundations for Static Dealer Site
**Learning:** Even in high-performance or visual-heavy sites (like those with radar animations), the "invisible" UX of accessibility (ARIA labels, current page states, live regions) is critical for a professional, inclusive experience.
**Action:** Always audit navigation links for `aria-current="page"`, external links for "opens in new tab" labels, and decorative animations for `aria-hidden`. Ensure mobile menu toggles synchronize their labels (`Open` vs `Close`) with their visual state.
